This company processes financial transactions for thousands of clients worldwide. Every API endpoint encodes financial logic: initiating transfers, approving transactions, and managing accounts across isolated tenants. The key challenge was how to test it at the speed and depth the platform demands.The solution wasn't delivered as an off-the-shelf product. This company became our design partner on the AI pentesting product and directly shaped how it was built.
